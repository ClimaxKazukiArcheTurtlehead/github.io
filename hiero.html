Climax Kazuki Arche Turtlehead e-sorts Gaming is a semi-professional gaming team produced and run by volunteer gamers. The team slogan is "Enjoy as much as you can, and if you gather even the heads of turtles that are sluggish and small, they will be as big and reliable as an ark", and the names of the teams are also adopted from each. We aim to be a team that is loved and supported by all people, and that we will develop bonds within the team through exchange games, competitions, and daily practice, and that our in-game skills will continue to grow mentally. Climax Kazuki Arche Turtlehead e-sorts Gaming is a new category of e-sports team, founded on February 21, 2021 and whose market has continued to expand in recent years. In the early days of climax kazuki arche turtlehead esorts gaming, three divisions were established: Clash Royale, which is a worldwide hit, Brow Stars, and the pinnacle of the quiz app. Kamenoko, Kino, Yazun, Rocket, Koi-san, and the owner Umeyoshi belonged to each of them as initial members.
